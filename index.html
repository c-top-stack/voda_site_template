About Us

Know more about us

  • Voda Paints Limited is a wholly-owned Nigerian company incorporated in 2004 as a Private Limited Liability Company.
  • The company is engaged in the manufacturing and marketing of wide range of high quality decorative and industrial range of paints, coatings, finishes and related products and services.
  • Voda Paints Limited is a registered member of the Paint Manufacturers Association of Nigeria, Nigerian Chamber of Commerce and Standards Organisation of Nigeria (SON) Mandatory Conformity Assessment Programme (MANCAP) certified.

With a staff strength of over fifty and installed capacity of 100,000 litres/day 7days a week backed up with the latest colour tinting and paint technology systems, the company is strongly positioned to continue its commitment towards excellent services and products delivery to its clients nationwide.
